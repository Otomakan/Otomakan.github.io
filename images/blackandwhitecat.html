GIF EDITOR Pimp your GIF with stickers and captions! More effects coming soon!

Follow these easy steps to add stickers or captions to any GIF:

STEP 1 START WITH A GIF

Choose a GIF to get started by entering a GIF URL or any GIPHY link or by dragging and dropping your image files.
PRO TIP: You can also start with a still image and add stickers or animated captions to make a GIF!


STEP 2 ADD STICKERS

On the Stickers tab, search our entire sticker collection to add funny or weird GIF stickers to your GIF. Just type in your search term or click on our suggested searches, and click on the sticker you want to add to your GIF. You can add as many stickers as you want!
PRO TIP: Try some of our favorite sticker searches: skeleton, Kanye, dinosaur.


STEP 3 POSITION YOUR STICKERS

You can reposition, resize, or remove your sticker by hovering over it in the GIF preview.


STEP 4 ADD FILTERS

Click the Filters tab, choose a filter, and decide whether to apply it to the whole background or the entire GIF.

STEP 5 ADD CAPTION TEXT

Click the Caption tab, then type your witty, relevant, or irrelevant caption text into the Caption field.



STEP 6 CUSTOMIZE YOUR CAPTION

Optional: Choose a style, color, and animation for your caption. Resize or reposition it by hovering over it and dragging within the preview.


STEP 7 CREATE GIF

Hit the Create GIF button to finish. You will be redirected to the GIF page when it is complete.


STEP 8 SHARE!

You can share your GIF on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, Tumblr, Reddit, text message, email, and everywhere!

AND YOU'RE DONE! Wasn't that so easy?
