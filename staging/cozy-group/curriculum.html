Menu

Infant (6 weeks old - 2 years old)

  • The infants' schedule is determined based on individual development and needs.
  • Infants eat and sleep on demand as needed throughout the day.
  • Infants are diapered every two hours or as needed.

    • Toddler / Preschooler (24 months - 5 years old)

  • 6:00 a.m. - 8:00 a.m. Drop off / Health check / Hand washing free play
  • 8:30 a.m. - 9:00 a.m. Handwashing / Breakfast
  • 9:00 a.m. - 9:30 a.m. Clean-up / Bathroom / Diapering
  • 9:30 a.m. - 9:45 a.m. Circle time / story
  • 9:45 a.m. - 10:30 a.m. Center time / Activity
  • 10:30 a.m. - 11:15 a.m. Movement / Outdoors
  • 11:15 a.m. - 11:30 a.m. Music
  • 11:30 a.m. - 12:00 p.m. Handwashing / Lunch time
  • 12:00 p.m. - 12:30 p.m. Clean-up / bathroom/diapering
  • 12:30 p.m. - 2:30 p.m. Rest time / Quiet time
  • 2:30 p.m. - 2:45 p.m. Waking up / bathroom/diapering
  • 2:45 p.m. - 3:00 p.m. Afternoon snack/Clean-up
  • 3:00 p.m. - 3:15 p.m. Story time
  • 3:15 p.m. - 3:45 p.m. Activity time
  • 3:45 p.m. - 4:00 p.m. Music / Movement
  • 4:00 p.m. - 4:45 p.m. Handwashing / Dinner
  • 4:45 p.m. - 5:30 p.m. Center time / Clean-up
  • 5:30 p.m. - 6:00 p.m. Pick up / Table games

    • Food

    Our program will provide daily breakfast, lunch, snack, and dinner. Under the Child and Adult Care Food Program and following regulations and guidelines of the New York Department of Health. Food from home will not be permitted into the daycare, otherwise instructed by a physician.
    * A physician's note is required.
    * Our child care program supports breastfeeding.
